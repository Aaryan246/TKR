Vision

Team Kaiser Racing is the collegiate BAJA team of Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues Institute of Technology, Navi Mumbai which was founded in 2017 with the goal of participating in the SAE BAJA Competition. The team consists of young passionate and dedicated students who design, built, test, promote and race an All-Terrian vehicle that can withstand the harshest elements of rough terrain.
